Tinder
The Original Swipe Machine

Tinder pioneered the swipe, and it has barely evolved since 2012. The algorithm shows you faces. You swipe yes or no. That is it. No depth, no compatibility, no context.

  • ✗ Matches on appearance only
  • ✗ Zero personality compatibility
  • ✗ Designed to maximize swipes, not matches

Bumble
Same Algorithm, Different Wrapper

Bumble made women message first, which is genuinely thoughtful. But underneath? The same photo-first, swipe-based system with a pink coat of paint and zero compatibility science.

  • ✗ Still entirely appearance-driven
  • ✗ No emotional compatibility layer
  • ✗ Bio prompts are surface-level at best
  • ✗ No deeper profiling or chart analysis

"Designed To Be Deleted" - But Is It?

Hinge claims it wants you to find love and delete the app. Noble. But its matching is still based on likes, location, and prompt responses. Not deep psychological or astrological alignment.

  • ✗ Still primarily image-based matching
